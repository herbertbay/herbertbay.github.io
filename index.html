About Herbert Bay

A visionary entrepreneur and globally recognized expert in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Lead-inventor of the groundbreaking SURF algorithm, co-founder of six AI ventures, and recipient of prestigious awards including the ECCV Koenderink Prize. Beyond technology, an adventurer who crossed both the Atlantic and Pacific oceans on a sailboat with his family.

Key Achievements

Entrepreneur & Investor

  • Co-founder of seven Swiss/US AI ventures:
    • Mia21 - Current Position, Build Better Bots
    • Earkick - Current Position, tracking mental health
    • PMF Engine - Find product market fit using AI
    • kooaba - First cloud-based image recognition (exit 2014 to Qualcomm)
    • Shortcut - Secure notes app
    • Inspiced - AI-powered cooking app
    • Waypoint.li - Mapping app for sailors (discontinued)
  • Driving innovation with AI at companies such as Ifolor where I led Captural as their CEO and co-initiator
  • Investing in AI companies such as Let's Enhance
  • Coaching AI companies such as ethonAI

Computer Vision & ML

  • Lead-inventor of the SURF algorithm cited nearly 40,000 times in scientific publications
  • Received the 2016 ECCV - Koenderink Prize given to fundamental contributions in computer vision that stood the test of time
  • Received the 2011 CVIU Most Cited Paper Award, and many more...
  • Building and leading Computer Vision and Machine Learning teams at companies such as Magic Leap

Board Member & Advisor

  • Member of the board of directors at Ifolor 2019 - 2023
  • Co-founder and member of the board of directors at 47nord
  • Investor and advisor to US-based AI company Let's Enhance
  • Previously advising Tokyo-based AR / SLAM startup Kudan
  • Previously advising Moscow-based mapping startup Maps.me (acquired Nov 2014 by Mail.Ru)

Adventurer

  • Crossed the Atlantic and the Pacific ocean on a sailboat with my wife and our two wonderful boys 2 and 4 years old when we left
  • More adventures coming soon, stay tuned :-)
